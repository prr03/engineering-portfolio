1. Overview

This capstone project was completed over the course of a semester at the University of Oklahoma during the Spring 2026 semester. It was assigned as a four-person engineering team, where I participated as a Hardware Developer. The other roles were Project Manager and Software Developer.

The goal of this project was to develop a drive-by-wire system for a 2015 Nissan Leaf for the Mobility Intelligence Lab at the University of Oklahoma. To do so we had to intercept and replace the signals for the accelerating, braking, and steeering subsystems. A PS5 controller was used as user interface and a large switch was used as an emergency stop.
