About

My name is Paxton Rivers, and I am a recent graduate from the University of Oklahoma. I have a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.

I have a wide variety of interests and ambitions, but most of my projects are within hardware, software, embedded systems, PCB design, computer achitechture, and user interfaces. I enjoy understanding system behavior from the ground up, whether that means designing custom solutions, writing low-level firmware, or avoiding library usage in learning environments.

This site acts as a portfolio for my favorite engineering projects and includes the learning, design decisions, and testing that went into each project.
